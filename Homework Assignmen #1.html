How TO PREPARE SPAGHETTI

INGREDIENTS

  • Spaghetti
  • Onions
  • Vegetable oil
  • Tomato paste
  • Spices
    • Brown Pepper
    • Curry Powder
    • Ginger Powder
  • Mixed Vegetables
    • Red Pepper
    • Green Pepper
    • Carrots
  • Salt
  • Water
  • Chicken pieces

Steps:

  1. Boil water in a saucepan.
  2. Add salt.
  3. Add Spaghetti and allow to cook till tender.
      4. >This will take about 7-8 minutes. Do not over cook.
  4. To Another pan,add a quater cup of vegetable oil.
  5. Allow to heat up slightly and then add the Chicken pieces
  6. Add salt and brown pepper.
  7. Leave to fry until Golden brown.
  8. Set the chicken aside from the pan.
  9. Add Onions to the pan with the oil.
  10. Add the spices.
  11. Stir for 1 minute.
  12. Add Tomato paste and stir for more 3 minutes.
  13. Add Chicken pieces and stir to combine.
  14. Leave to fry for 8-10 minutes.
  15. Add little water.
  16. Transfer the Spaghetti to the pan with the other ingredients
  17. Mix to cmobine.
  18. Addthe mixed vegetables.
  19. Allow to cook for final 2 minutes.
  20. Serve with an Ice-cold Coca-Cola.

    21. BON APPETIT!!!
